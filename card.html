Cat

A group of kittens is called a "kindle."

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12-18 years
  • Habitat: Rural areas, generally cats can be found in the same locations we live. However, some larger cats will live in harsher climates

The cat comes in many colors and sizes! They are great as a home pet and make great companions for living comfortably inside.
